Echoes In The Abyss

We're rock, ska and jazz band that promises to take you on a dynamic and energetic musical journey that blends the soulful melodies of jazz, the upbeat rhythms of ska, and the raw power of rock. Let's start scrolling and learn more about us.

About Us

Echoes in the Abyss is a dynamic and diverse band that is known for its captivating live performances and genre-bending sound. Led by the powerful vocals of Aldo, our music is characterized by the unique fusion of rock, jazz, and blues that we bring to the stage. With the impeccable skills of guitarists Timotius and Reiji, our music is infused with soaring solos and intricate riffs that will leave you breathless. Bima's steady bass lines serve as the backbone of our sound, while Ditya's masterful piano playing adds an element of sophistication and complexity to our compositions. And let's not forget about Dewa, our fearless drummer who keeps the beat steady and the energy high throughout our shows. Together, we are Echoes in the Abyss, a band that is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of music and creating a unique and unforgettable experience for our audience.

Achievement

Soundsation

Winner of Soundsation• March 2021

Echoes in the Abyss has recently been crowned as the winner of the highly acclaimed music competition, Soundsation. Competing against some of the most talented artists in the industry, our band emerged victorious, thanks to our dynamic and innovative sound, and our electrifying stage presence. Soundsation is known for its rigorous selection process, which includes multiple rounds of auditions and performances. But we were undaunted by the challenge, and we poured our hearts and souls into every aspect of our music, from the writing and composition to the rehearsals and performances.

Knotfest

Performer• November 2022

Echoes in the Abyss had the honor of being the opening act for one of the biggest names in heavy metal: Slipknot. Our band was invited to perform at Knotfest, the annual music festival founded by Slipknot, and we were thrilled to have the opportunity to share the stage with such legendary performers. Sharing the stage with Slipknot was an experience we will never forget. Their professionalism and dedication to their craft was truly inspiring, and we were honored to be part of such an incredible event. We hope to continue pushing the boundaries of music and sharing our passion with audiences all over the world, just like Slipknot has done for decades.

Greatest Hits

Rhythm and Reflections• January 2020

We are thrilled to announce that our latest album, "Rhythm and Reflections," has sold an incredible 100,000 copies in just one month! This is a truly remarkable achievement for our band and a testament to the hard work and dedication that went into creating this album. From the writing and composition to the recording and production, we poured our hearts and souls into every aspect of "Rhythm and Reflections." And it seems that our fans have taken notice, responding with overwhelming support and enthusiasm for our latest release. This milestone is a huge accomplishment for our band, and it motivates us to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in music. We are humbled and grateful for the support of our fans, and we look forward to sharing our music with even more people in the months and years to come.

Single

Rumble in the Abyss• April 2020

"Rumble in the Abyss" is a high-energy, adrenaline-fueled rock anthem that showcases Echoes in the Abyss's talent for creating powerful, memorable music. With its driving rhythm section, soaring guitar solos, and catchy hooks, this song is a true crowd-pleaser that has resonated with audiences around the world. The fact that "Rumble in the Abyss" has been nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Young Indie Rock is a testament to the band's skill and dedication. This accolade recognizes the band's ability to create innovative, genre-defying music that pushes the boundaries of what's possible in rock music.We are grateful to the fans and critics who have embraced our music, and we look forward to continuing to create exciting and boundary-pushing music in the years to come.
